How do I determine the state of a toggled element?

You can determine whether an elemnt is collapsed or not by using the ":visible" and ":hidden" selectors.

var isVisible = $("#myDiv").is(":visible"); var isHidden = $("#myDiv").is(":hidden");

If you're simply acting on an elemnt based on its visibility, just include ":visible" or ":hidden" in the selector expression. For example:

$( "#myDiv:visible" ).animate({ left: "+=200px" }, "slow");
